  # Declaration to Triple Nuclear Energy


  * This Declaration recognizes the key role of nuclear energy in achieving
  global net-zero greenhouse gas emissions by 2050 as increasing the share of
  nuclear energy is critical to reaching the goal of limiting global temperature
  increase to 1.5 degrees Celsius from pre-industrial times.

  * Signatory countries: USA, Bulgaria, Canada, Czech Republic, Finland, France,
  Ghana, Hungary, Japan, South Korea, Moldova, Mongolia, Morocco, Netherlands,
  Poland, Romania, Slovakia, Slovenia, Ukraine, UAE, and the UK.
